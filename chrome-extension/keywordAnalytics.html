Keyword Performance

How to understand this chart

The x-axis indicates the average CPC for each keyword. (This is different from the bid, and indicates the actual amount you paid on average for these clicks.)

The y-axis indicates the number of clicks for each keyword.

The size of the dot indicates how many impressions each keyword has gotten.

The color of the dot indicates the average cost of a sale, with green indicating lower cost-of-sale and red higher. Keywords which have not recorded any sales are colored gray.
