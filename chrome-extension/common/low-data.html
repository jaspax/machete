Why don't I see any data?

AMS Unlocked can only gather data from the moment it is installed. If you have just installed, then try coming back tomorrow---you should see hour-by-hour updated data starting from right now.

AMS Unlocked also runs in your browser, so it can't gather data when any of the following are true:

  • Your computer is turned off
  • You are not signed in to Amazon Marketing Services.
  • You have closed your browser and have disabled the setting which allows Chrome to run apps in the background.
  • You do not have a working internet connection.

Finally, it's possible that your campaign just hasn't changed in the time frame you're looking at.
