Welcome to Machete! You're on your way to slashing through the jungles of Amazon Marketing Services and finding the city of gold.

How do I start?

Using Machete with AMS is a complicated 2-step process:

  1. Log in to Amazon Marketing Services.
  2. There is no second step.

What does it do?

On the campaign dashboard, Machete adds a chart icon to every cell. Click on it to see a view of how that metric has changed over time. (Note that it may take a few days before these charts have much data.)

On the campaign details page, Machete adds a new tab for Keyword Analytics, which gives you a visual representation of your keywords' impressions, clicks, and ACOS.

You also get a number of reports showing your best and worst keywords, with the ability to modify those keywords individually or in bulk.

Finally, Machete gives you a full-featured chart showing your campaign's performance history on a number of different metrics.
