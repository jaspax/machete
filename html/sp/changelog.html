Machete just got better

Version 1.3

  • The new Aggregate History tab lets you view the history for multiple campaigns simultaneously. Select one or more campaigns from the select box and view all of their history statistics together.
  • The Aggregate Keyword tab likewise combines keyword reports from multiple sources into a single place, letting you view and modify keywords from across multiple campaigns.
  • The Campaign History view now lets you view stats by week and by month, rather than just by day.
  • ...and other smaller bug fixes and improvements.

Version 1.2

  • Product Display Ads are now fully supported!. Check out the "Campaign History" tab after clicking on your campaign. Since Product Display Ads don't have keywords, they don't have keyword analytics.
  • Select specific date ranges on the Campaign History tab to see exacty what your metrics were on that date and see how your campaign has changed in the selected time range.
  • Download the complete history of your campaign as a .csv file for offline analysis. (Notice how cool my hand-drawn red circle is.)
  • The Keyword Analytics bubble chart has been revamped to better represent the most important keyword metrics.
  • ... and a number of other smaller enhancements.

Version 1.1

  • Bulk update all keywords in your campaign from the keyword table.
  • Visit your Machete profile from the link in the top bar.
  • Paused and completed campaigns no longer count against your quota for free and pro subscriptions.
  • A number of smaller bugfixes, performance improvements, and security tweaks.

Version 1.0
